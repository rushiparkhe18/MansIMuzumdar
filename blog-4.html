How to Know If You’re Ready

If you feel an inner call to explore your soul’s records, you’re likely ready for a reading.

Types of Readings

  • Channeled Readings: Connect with higher beings for guidance on specific questions.
  • Soul Readings: Explore your soul’s blueprint using intuition.

Key Insights from a Reading

  • Energy Center: Understand your soul’s primary energy center.

Benefits of an Akashic Record Reading

  • Life Clarity: Understand your soul’s purpose and direction.
  • Healing & Growth: Gain insights to heal past traumas and grow spiritually.
  • Manifestation Power: Align with your highest potential to manifest abundance.
